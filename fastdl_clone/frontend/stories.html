How to Download Instagram Stories?

  1. Open the Instagram Story you want to save.
  2. Copy the link (Tap 3 dots -> Copy Link).
  3. Paste it here and click Download.

Is it Anonymous?

Yes. When you use Instaminsta, our server views the story, not you. The user will never know you watched or downloaded their story.
